Welcome to Assi Linkedin, a personal project developed by Shankha Dey. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use my app. I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled responsibly.

Information Collection

Assi Linkedin may collect the following information:

I do not collect sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary for the app’s operation.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, if any.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties unless required by law. Your data is only shared with LinkedIn as part of the authentication process, per their terms.

Data Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your data, including storing it securely and limiting access. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights
